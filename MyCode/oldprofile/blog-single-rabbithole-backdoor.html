How does privacy and proprietary software play a unneeded role and sometimes a threat in a threat landscape area?

  • Jimmy Neutron
  • Web Design
  • 14

Privacy and Proprietary software play a large role in every day life , and in the new 21st century , are a large part of many disruptions at many levels of both society , government , and even the military. Have you ever gotten a forced update you didnt appreciate ? Ever wanted admin control on a device you purchased? Have you ever felt less than adequate , or like you are always talking to a bot ?

It's because companies are allowed to design it that way , and its best for their profit. -

Under a BIG change to current government laws , with NEW BETTER government laws , we could fix this. Although many days today. Companies and people alike are left in the dark when it comes to used and abused DRM software.

DRM , may have a place in some roles , but any sort or form of pay - enhancement or pay walls , or restrictions or alteratiojns of any kind involving money , after a transaction is made , is and should be HIGHLY illegal

Any regular user , company , or professional today should ask themselves about there own security individually and ask , " DO I have Full Admin control ? or full hardware control ? "

Everyone nowadays that uses a device , should advocate in their contry by FIRST asking themselves these questions.

  1. Should I petition against my governmentabout selling devices to the public that do not have full
  2. Do I or does my tech professional have full access to every byte of data ? If the answer is no , that's a HUGE security risk.
  3. Does your business share data with another business ? --- This is only something that should be allowed by people who do not make much money.
  4. Does your new device have less features and is harder to use than before ? - This is a common tactic
  5. Does your device contain pre made bloatware , adware , and virus's that slow down your phone over time ?
  6. Do you want full control over all of your devices , with the option of ALL features being made by freewre , courtesy of a government , instead of insider trading with monopolies ?
  7. Does your elections systems , or , political systems use any form of windows or microsoft products? --- If so , your political system may be under attack from these viral companies.

Technology is advancing yet , peoples overall capability is declining. No one is out teaching anybody anything properly. it is a mess out there in the public view. Some Instituitons are losing some placeholder values , that should not be valid or accepted. Point being , what do we do about this? Do we worry about the next covid and hide under our beds ? No no no , I am afraid as a security expert I must tell you , at TechSport , We HIGHLY advise AGANST doing that in any way.

You can teach and educate yourself some, but most importantly teach the youth and spread the message about freeware. Advertisements cna cause horrible mental damage , emotional stress , and a waste of brain cell usage. Adversitements , either unwanted , - like amazon deciding to change a contract and ad advertisements , or anything else , should be immediately sued as malice. A person or a citizen with RIGHTS should not need a large lawyer to do this , do it in smaller courts if need be.
