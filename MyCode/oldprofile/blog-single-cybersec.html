Cybersecurity commandments and guidelines

  • Jason London
  • Web Design
  • 14

Virus's are easily defined and recognizeable but not as easily removable , due to the corporations who create them . Adware , bloat ware , and any form in the slightest bit of aiding and ebedding advertisement , past the original agreement , is considered not only software compliant perjury , but is also a huge security risk . Trust of Trust , is explained like this. I want you to mail me the keys and title to your paid for vehicle right now. What would you say ? You would say no correct? What if i said it was for your own good ? What if I made them electric and stole ALL of your control away , from you and all of your childeren forever , would that be okay ? No. Any form of adware , bloatware , forced updates , forced update drivers , unagreed advertisement , or unreleasal of function , for money , A.K.A , Pay wall games , will noe need to be banned , and around the TechSport company I work at , they will be as per the boss's orders.

Rule #1.

" If you cant see every bit and have full admin access to that , How in the hell should you expect someone to secure it? - Whatever you do , don't be a sellout idiot with your business , and choose windowws updates. ". Countless business's are being exploited ravishly that run on windows machines. There are things we cna do such as , close ports , turn off unneeded servies , do a security audit , But in the end , we will really need to use a network managament system to relieve the viral background connections , if you or your business uses windows."

Rule # 2 , an online application , an out of house application , a third party app , or ESPECIALLY , an application that one cannot fully see or manage the code on , is a HUGE security risk.

Rule # 3. Admin rights , are the same as human rights , guaranteed by the U.N. Most of the people we have spoken with , say they are ready to protest over it , as they still do not have admin access on mobile devices ( managed through google ) , or on windows ( windows update with intel firmware. ).

We believe in power to the people , or to the users , as in 100% full control , or it is technological slavery. These truths , such as many before us are obviously self- evident.
